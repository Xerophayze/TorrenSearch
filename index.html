Jackett and Prowlarr unlock many more trackers through one local API. Direct browser access depends on their CORS settings; public CORS proxies usually cannot reach your private localhost services.

qBittorrent control uses the saved WebUI URL and credentials from the TorrenSearch backend. Use the URL that the TorrenSearch backend can reach. On the Synology Gluetun setup, that is often http://127.0.0.1:8080, not the external VPN-forwarded address. Keep this app private to your LAN or VPN.

Public proxy presets are best-effort only. A provider can be ready before search and still fail if that proxy blocks the target site. For reliable multi-provider results, run the local TorrenSearch proxy or your own hosted proxy.
